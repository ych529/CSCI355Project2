Healthy life

You can buy meat everywhere, we provide fresh vegetables everyday.

  • Vegetables are one of the essential foods in people's life. Vegetables can provide a variety of vitamins and minerals necessary for the human body. 90% of the vitamin C and 60% of the vitamin A necessary for the human body come from vegetables.
  • Eating more vegetables is of great benefit to our body. Vegetables contain many kinds of vitamins and proteins. If the human body lacks these vitamins, it will suffer from many diseases.
  • For a healthy person with normal food intake, 300 grams of potatoes, 400 grams of cabbage, cabbage, carrots, spinach, leeks, tomatoes or other vegetables a day is enough.
